Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

English · Español · Français

Arbitro ("the app") is a betting-stake calculator. This policy explains what data the app handles and, just as importantly, what it does not.

No account, no personal data collection

Arbitro does not require an account, login, or any personal information to use. The app does not operate any server that stores or receives your data.

What's stored, and where

The odds, budgets, and calculation history you enter are stored only on your device, using standard local app storage. This data is never transmitted to us or to any third party. You can delete it at any time from the app's Settings screen ("Clear history") or by uninstalling the app.

In-app purchases

Arbitro offers an optional one-time "Pro" purchase. This purchase is processed entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play — we never see or store your payment details. Their own privacy policies govern that transaction:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

No analytics, no ads, no tracking

The app does not integrate analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking tools.

Children's privacy

Arbitro is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, since it collects no personal data at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update will be posted at this same address with a revised "Last updated" date.
