Need help with Arbitro? Start here — most issues are quick fixes.

I bought Pro, but it still shows locked

Open Settings inside the app and tap "Restore Purchases." This asks Apple to re-confirm your purchase without charging you again.

How do I clear my saved history?

Open Settings → "Clear history." This cannot be undone.

Is my data backed up anywhere?

No — everything (odds, budgets, saved history) is stored only on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes it permanently.
